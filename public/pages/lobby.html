Ready to Trade?
You'll be matched into a table of 6 players. The session starts automatically once the table fills up.

Interface

  • All registered players are divided into groups of 8.
  • 6 real players + 2 bots share one trading table.
  • Everyone can see the current question, holdings, and cash of all players at the table.

Game Framework

  • Six digits (1–9) are generated at the start of the game and stay hidden through all 6 rounds.
  • The true asset value is the sum of all six digits, revealed only at the end.
  • PnL accumulates across rounds — it is never reset to zero mid-game.

Question Phase

  • One question appears at the start of every round.
  • The correct answer is the hidden digit for that round.
  • Keep your answer to yourself — that's your edge.

Assets & Ownership

  • The value of one asset equals the sum of all six digits.
  • Each player starts with 2 assets.
  • One bonus asset is given after round 3.
  • There is no limit on maximum holdings.

Trading Rules

  • Quote a bid and/or ask price after working out the question.
  • Each round has a 45 second trading window.
  • Maximum price is 100.
  • One buy order AND one sell order allowed per round, per player.
  • Each order covers exactly one asset.

Trading Logic

  • If the highest bid is below the lowest ask, no trade executes.
  • If the highest bid meets or beats the lowest ask, a trade executes.
  • The clearing price is the midpoint of the best bid and best ask.
  • Orders are matched on price-time priority.
  • No self-trading is allowed.
  • The sum of all players' PnL is always zero.

Bots

  • 2 bots join every table automatically.
  • Bots quote around the true value with some randomness, mainly to keep the market liquid.
  • Bot PnL is never counted toward the leaderboard.

End of Round

  • The true asset value is revealed at game end.
  • Final wealth = cash + (asset count − 3) × true value.
  • Players are ranked by total PnL across all games played.
